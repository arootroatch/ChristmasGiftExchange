Gift Exchange Generator

For families who draw names for their holiday gift exchange, here's a web app to make it easier!

Step 1: Enter the names of everyone participating in the gift exchange. Make sure all names are unique. If two people have the same name, please add a last initial or alternate spelling of their name.

Step 2 (optional): To stop some people from getting each others' names, add households to group them together. You can drag and drop to move people around or select their name from the drop down in each box. Names in the top box will be matched to anyone.

Step 3: Click "Generate List" and watch it go!
